Pasta Carbonara

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make this Italian classic with a twist

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of bacon
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces of dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley
  • ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until crisp and drain on paper towels
  2. In a medium bowl, beat together the eggs and the cream. Stir in the cheese and set aside
  3. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the package. Drain and return to the pan. Toss with butter until melted. Add the bacon and cheese mixture
