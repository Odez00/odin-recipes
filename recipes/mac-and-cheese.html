Man and Cheese

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make the ultimate comfort food, Mac and Cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 8 ounces of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 ounces of shredded Swiss cheese
  • 4 ounces of Velveeta processed cheese
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the elbow macaroni in the boiling water for about 8 minutes and drain
  3. Melt the butter in an oven proof pan. Add the flour and stir over heat until the flour begins to brown. Take it off heat and stir in about 1 cup of milk until fully incorporated. Put back on the heat and simmer until it begins to thicken. Add in the rest of the milk
  4. Add all of the cheeses and stir until melted. Once the cheeses have melted add in the macaroni
  5. Cover the top with panko crumbs and place the pan in the oven. Bake for abut 20 minutes or until bubbling
